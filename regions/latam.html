The Landless Workers' Movement (MST) in Brazil has launched a National Day of Struggles in Defense of Agrarian Reform 2026, marking 30 years since the Eldorado do Carajás massacre. The movement also observes April 17 as the International Day of Peasant Struggles, with actions including tree planting in memory of global peasant struggles.
