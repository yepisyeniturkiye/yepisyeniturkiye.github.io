### 5. Afrikalı yolculuk paylaşım firması YANGO, NEV mobilitesi için FUTURE CAR ile ortaklık kurdu

Heyet, Zhejiang Jixin Kaynak Geri Dönüşüm Teknolojisi dahil ekosistem ortaklarını inceledi.

*YANGO, one of Africa's largest ride-hailing platforms, visited Hangzhou with fleet partners from Ghana and partnered with FUTURE CAR to set a benchmark for new energy vehicle (NEV) mobility in Africa. The delegation inspected ecosystem partners including Zhejiang Jixin Resource Recycling Technology.*
